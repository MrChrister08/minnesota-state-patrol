Traffic Enforcement

The Minnesota State Patrol enforces traffic laws to ensure the safety of all road users. This includes speed enforcement, DWI enforcement, and seatbelt enforcement.

Commercial Vehicle Enforcement

Our Commercial Vehicle Section ensures that commercial vehicles comply with state and federal regulations to maintain safety on the roadways.

Specialized Units

We have specialized units such as the Aviation Section, Special Response Team, and Crash Reconstruction Unit that provide additional support and expertise.
