Safety Tips

Follow these tips to stay safe on the road: always wear your seatbelt, obey speed limits, never drive under the influence of alcohol or drugs, and stay focused on driving without distractions.

Programs & Initiatives

We run various programs such as Towards Zero Deaths (TZD) and Safe and Sober to reduce traffic fatalities and serious injuries on Minnesota roads.
